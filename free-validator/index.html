The 1-Page Business Idea Validator

Most business ideas fail because they were never tested before money was spent.

This 1-page worksheet runs your idea through five sections — idea statement, target customer, top 3 competitors, initial cost estimate, and a 3-question Go / No-Go rule.

What’s inside

  • 01 — Idea Statement. One sentence. If it doesn’t fit on one line, your idea is still too fuzzy.
  • 02 — Target Customer. If you can’t picture one specific person, you don’t have a customer yet.
  • 03 — Top 3 Competitors + Price Range. Market check before you sink cash.
  • 04 — Initial Cost Estimate. Five line items + a 15% cushion. Round up.
  • 05 — Go / No-Go Decision. Three questions. The NO answers are the most useful.

How to use it

  1. Print one copy per idea you’re considering.
  2. Fill it in by hand. Writing forces clarity that typing doesn’t.
  3. Apply the decision rule:
    • 3 YES = GO. Build the full plan this week.
    • 2 YES = WAIT. Fix the one NO before spending any money.
    • 0-1 YES = NOT YET. Re-validate this idea in 30 days.

After you validate
